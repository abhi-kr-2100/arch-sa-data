ArchSAIso is an open-access databank of isotope values from South Asian Archaeological researches.

Isotope values are widely used in archaeological research to determine ancient diet, place of origin, immigration pattern, lifestyle, etc. The aim of this project is to compile an open-access web-based databank of isotope values, along with the associated contextual variablesâ€”site location, nature of archaeological material, context and references, etc., from published South Asian archaeological research papers.

This project hopes to provide researchers with an online platform where they can share isotope values from their own published studies, as well as freely access data on isotope values and related information for their own research.
