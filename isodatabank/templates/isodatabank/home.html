South Asian Archaeological Isotope Data is an open-access databank of isotope values from South Asian Archaeological researches.

We provide researchers free access to high-quality published isotope data from various South Asian Archaeological studies and researches in simple-to-use Excel format. All important contextual variables, such as site location, description, information about original authors, as well as direct links to the original research papers are also provided.

Additionally, researchers looking to share high-quality published isotope data from South Asian Archaeological sites, can host their research data on this website.
